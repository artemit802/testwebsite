Pigeons are amazing creatures that are often misunderstood. Despite their reputation as pests, Pigeons are highly intelligent and resourceful birds that can thrive in a variety of environments. With their iridescent feathers and soft coos, pigeons have captivated people and were even used as messengers and carriers during wars. In many cities, pigeons can be found on buildings and statues, their gentle gait and pecking crumbs are a common sight. With their remarkable homing abilities and complex social behavior, pigeons are a true wonder of nature and deserve recognition and respect for their a unique place in the urban ecosystem.
